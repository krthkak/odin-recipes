Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a breaded chicken breast topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella, Parmesan, or provolone cheese. It is a popular Italian-American dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place flour in a shallow dish. Place eggs in another shallow dish. In a third shallow dish, combine bread crumbs and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
  3. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, then dip in eggs, and coat with bread crumb mixture.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden and crispy, about 3 minutes per side.
  5. Place chicken in a baking dish. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 20 minutes. Sprinkle with fresh basil and additional Parmesan cheese before serving.
